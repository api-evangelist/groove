This comparison evaluates three AI support tools for B2B teams in 2026. Drift AI, a sales tool being sunset by Clari and Salesloft, is contrasted with Chatbase, which charges per message and risks inaccurate responses, and Helply, which offers a free helpdesk with outcome-based pricing starting at $0.25 per drafted reply.
